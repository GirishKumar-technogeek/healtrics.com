Problems Solved By Healtrics

Major Healthcare Problems

  •  Interaction with multiple apps in the existing healthtech & healthcare ecosystem for pre treatment, post treatment, insurance claims
  •  Diversified Quality , More time , More Human Effort, More Costs
  •  Replication , Out Of Sync Medical Records
  •  Have to go through a tedious process for second opinions
  •  Ambiguous and traditional methods of healthcare data storage and appointment maintenance
  •  High Pharmacy costs and targeted medicine availability in pharmacies
  •  Low Doctor to Patient ratio , Low Paramedical Staff to Patient ratio
  •  Medical Data Leakage , Less Availability of required data right time at right place
  •  Low Bed to Patient Ratio
  •  Less home healthcare , rural healthcare penetration
  •  Manual Interpretation needed for obtaining valuable insights due to omnibus nature of healthcare data collection
  •  No Location based availability of emergency health needs (such as blood, oxygen, beds)

How Healtrics solves this problem?

A technology led, fully integrated cloud based web, mobile and desktop healthcare aggregation platform which provides holistic ecosystems to patients, doctors, paramedical staff, hospitals, clinics with high quality, high security and seamlessly integrated features and user experience. We aim at providing best in class, personalized, multi channel, specialized services to all users. Our platform provides features such as AI Enabled Suggestions, AI Enabled Assistance, AI Enabled Video Consultation, Patient Feedback Maintenance, Online Doctor Appointments, Medical/Surgical Procedure Aggregation, Risk Profiling, Block chain based Electronic Health Records, Home Nursing, Home HDU, Remote Patient Monitoring, Home Doctor, Home Medical Equipment, Home Pharmacy & Diagnostics, Lab Tests Integration, Hospital Management Systems , Clinic Management System, Integrated Payment Service, Instant Medicine Delivery, family based subscriptions, health emergency services, IoT enabled rural health hubs. Other than this our pipeline of features will keep increasing based on our user feedbacks and increasing demand in the healthcare industry to provide a holistic experience.

How Healtrics is unique?

“Healtrics is where the future of healthtech is” is our vision. Healtrics is one of the early healthcare startups adopting “Pay As You Go” and “Pay As Per Your Use” B2B2C solutions in the healthcare industry. Patients don’t need to manage their accounts in multiple apps. We save the enormous human effort involved, more time involved, higher costs involved in various stages of treatment, consultation, insurance, medicine, lab tests etc. Healtrics is the platform solving Doctor to Patient ratio, Bed to Patient ratio and many other issues in the current healthcare industry through technology. We do help doctors, paramedical staff, clinics, hospitals through custom SaaS portals which helps in their day to day tasks such as appointment maintenance , administrative tasks, patient care, billing etc. Healtrics will be the next global preferred on the go holistic healthcare platform complying with all global standards of all healthcare associations and governments.

Why Should one choose Healtrics?

Healtrics is a one stop holistic healthcare platform which aims at solving the issues faced by patients, doctors, paramedical staff, hospitals, clinics all over the globe. I started working on this problem since our current healthcare industry faces issues such as healthcare data maintenance and storage, applying machine learning and data analysis to obtain valuable insights on the data gathered through the multi-channel services available currently, high healthcare and pharmaceutical costs, few technological implementations on emergency conditions and many more indirect issues. Healtrics as a platform revolves around the concepts “You take care of your health we take care of the rest” for patients and “You aim at providing the best healthcare to patients we take care of the rest of your problems”. We as a tech healthcare startup don’t want to separate patients from rural and urban. We provide best care to all patients and only the channel of delivery differs. Though Healtrics acts as a holistic platform, we do value patients' engagement and experience adding no pressure to them during their treatment.
